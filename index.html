Asian Noodle Soup

  1. Build your broth
    1. Add 6 quarts water
    2. Add 1.5 tblsps tamari
    3. Add dash sesame oil
    4. Add minced ginger
    5. Add minced garlic
    6. Add diced mushroom
    7. Add chopped scallions
    8. Add squirt umami stuff
    9. Add sesame seeds
    10. Add 1 tsp fish sauce
    11. Add 1 tblsp boullion
  2. Dice your veggies
  3. Cube the tofu
  4. Add udon noodle
  5. Add julienned veggies
  6. Bring water back to boil, cook two minutes
  7. Turn off heat, serve
  8. Yum that sauce!!!
